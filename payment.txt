For a financial services, advisory, and education platform based in India (which seems to be your target given the references to Pune and Ahmedabad in your story), you have a few excellent options for payment integration.

Since you offer services like consulting, courses, and premium advisory, you'll likely need a gateway that supports standard one-time payments, payment links (for consulting invoices), and possibly subscriptions (if you plan to offer recurring courses or retainers).

Here are the best payment integration options for your platform:

1. Razorpay (Highly Recommended)
Razorpay is currently the industry standard for Indian businesses and tech startups.

Why it fits your site: It provides a beautifully designed checkout experience that will match the premium feel of your website.
Key Features: Supports Payment Links (great for invoicing high-ticket consulting clients without needing a shopping cart), Subscriptions (for recurring advisory fees), and Smart Collect (virtual accounts for large NEFT/RTGS transfers from Ultra HNIs).
Integration: Extremely developer-friendly with excellent React/Node.js SDKs.
2. Cashfree Payments
A very strong alternative to Razorpay, especially known for good success rates and payouts.

Why it fits your site: If you ever need to disburse payments or handle complex splits (e.g., if you have partner consultants), Cashfree is excellent.
Key Features: Lowest settlement times, great UPI integration, and robust payment gateways for both domestic and international cards.
Integration: Easy to integrate with modern web frameworks.
3. Stripe (For International & Domestic)
If you anticipate having a lot of Non-Resident Indian (NRI) clients or international businesses consulting with you.

Why it fits your site: Stripe has the absolute best international card acceptance rate and provides the most "premium" feeling checkout experience globally.
Key Features: World-class APIs, seamless subscription management, and automatic localized pricing.
Integration: They provide pre-built optimized checkout components for React.
4. PayU / CCAvenue
Traditional but highly reliable payment gateways.

Why it fits your site: They have deep integrations with almost all Indian banks and offer excellent rates for enterprise businesses. However, their checkout UIs can sometimes feel slightly dated compared to Razorpay or Stripe.
